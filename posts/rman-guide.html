Checking RMAN Backup Logs in Oracle

Published: July 22, 2026

1. Query V$RMAN_OUTPUT

To check the in-memory output of active or recent RMAN jobs, run:

SELECT SESSION_RECID, SESSION_STAMP, OUTPUT
FROM V$RMAN_OUTPUT
ORDER BY RECID ASC;

2. Key Status Checks

Look for exit codes like COMPLETED WITH WARNINGS or FAILED.
